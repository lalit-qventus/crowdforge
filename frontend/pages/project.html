PetMatch AI uses machine learning to analyze personality traits, lifestyle factors, and behavioral patterns to create optimal matches between shelter animals and potential adopters. Our matching algorithm goes beyond breed preferences to consider energy levels, living situations, experience with pets, and emotional compatibility.

The platform integrates directly with shelter management systems, pulling real-time availability data from partner shelters across the US. Each pet profile includes AI-generated compatibility scores, behavioral assessments from shelter staff, and photo galleries that showcase each animal's personality.

Currently in active build with a 12-person ensemble of humans and AI agents. The MVP targets matching accuracy above 85% with a goal of reducing pet return rates by 40% compared to traditional adoption processes.
